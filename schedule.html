💡 Focus Tips
01
Work in 25-minute blocks

Use the Pomodoro technique — study for 25 mins, then take a 5-min break. Repeat 4 times, then take a longer break.

02
Silence notifications

Put your phone on Do Not Disturb and close unneeded browser tabs before starting a session.

03
Set a clear goal first

Before each session, pin one task to Today's Focus so you always know exactly what you're working on.

04
Keep water nearby

Staying hydrated keeps your brain alert. Avoid leaving your desk for water mid-session.
